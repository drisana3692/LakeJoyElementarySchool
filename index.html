Campus Life

The Classrooms

Teachers have the joy of learning here

I absolutely LOVE the classrooms because whenever I walk into one, I feel a mood where there us education and joy. In classrooms, we learn many many things!

The art room

Beautiful drawings and paintings!

We always do fun arts and crafts for us to take home and showcase! I remember we did a lot of coloring, butterflies, painting, and more!

The music room

Different varieties of music!

I love the music teacher, Mrs. Edwards. We always do fun activities. For instance, hot potato and music bingo. The teacher also allowed us to play our instruments in class. I played the violin.

The library

Many books to choose from!

The library never fails to impress me with a book. There are so many books to choose from; fiction, nonfiction, fantasy, humor, and more! We also get to take book quizzes to get points and win prizes!

Achievements

2010 Georgia School of Excellence in Student Achievement

The Georgia Schools of Excellence Program honors brilliant schools. Any school that is either academically superior or demonstrate dramatic gains in student achievement are awarded. These schools serve as role models for other schools throughout the state.

What's New

  • Renovated School Cafeteria

    The newly renovated cafeteria serves fresh food. The cafeteria is clean, with nice picnic tables. The cafeteria's walls are decorated in pretty lion stickers. The cafeteria is a nice shade of blue, representing a school color. Whenever you enter the cafeteria, you can see all of the students eating happily.

  • New School Playground

    At the playground, there are monkey bars, swings, and rock climbing walls. You can play with your friends, or even try the zipline! This playground is really cool!
